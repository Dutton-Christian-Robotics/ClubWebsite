Rover Simulator v1

The bot in this simulator can respond to commands to rotate its two wheels, enough to move the bot forward, backwards, and to turn. The stage backdrop is an empty screen without obstacles.

Rover Simulator v2

This is a more advanced simulation than v1. The bot includes an array of active and passive sensors, as well as a mechanical arm and claw. Multiple backdrops are provided, all surrounded by a purple wall; some include multiple obstacle walls as well.

Rover Simulator v2.5

An updated version of the simulator that eliminates the "SEEING RED" and "TOUCHING RED" sensors, adding a "SEEING COLOR" sensor in its place. Long-range sensors also now stop at any color. Also added a cargo capacity limitation for the rover and a green base sprite where hearts can be unloaded.
