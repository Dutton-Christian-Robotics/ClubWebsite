Rover v2 To Do List

To Do List

Use this list as a "what should I do first" and "what should I do next" list.

  • Decide what the bot should do when it's turned on (the flag is clicked)
    For example, do we just start moving forward? Do we want to make a random directional choice? Do we want to make a smart movement choice?
  • Get the bot to move forward
  • Get the bot to know when it's approaching a wall
  • Get the bot to decide what to do when it's near a wall
    You also need to decide—how close is too close?
  • Decide how to make the bot do more than just wander around.
    Do you implement random action? Intelligent action? How often do you do it? Hint: check out the timer variable.
  • Get the bot to know when it's looking at a heart
    How will this change its behavior? should it keep making random moves?
  • Get the bot to grab a heart when it touches one
